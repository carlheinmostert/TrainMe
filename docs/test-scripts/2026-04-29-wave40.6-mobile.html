Wave 40.6 · haptics deep fix + photo refresh + treatment thumbnails

Camera haptics + photo spinner + treatment-aware thumbnails

Three targeted fixes from Wave 40.5 device QA. (A) Haptics rewritten from UIImpactFeedbackGenerator to CHHapticEngine with playsHapticsOnly = true — fires reliably during active recording, on repeated calls, and across the full session. (B) Photo-last-exercise spinner race condition fixed by replacing the guard-stacking pattern with a monotonic sequence number + merge-based refresh. The conversion listener is now the sole authority for conversion state. (C) Studio card thumbnails now reflect the practitioner's preferred treatment: line-drawing, B&W (grayscale filter), or original (raw). 9 items in 3 sections.

A · Haptics (CHHapticEngine rewrite)

  • A1. Shutter press-down fires every time (not just first).
    1. Open any client → session → Camera mode.
    2. Tap the shutter quickly (photo capture). Feel the haptic tick.
    3. Wait a few seconds. Tap again. Confirm the haptic fires again.
    4. Repeat 4–5 more times across 30 seconds. Every tap should produce a haptic.
  • A2. Per-second ticks fire during video recording.
    1. Long-press the shutter to start a video recording.
    2. While recording (red dot visible), count 5 seconds. You should feel a haptic tick on each second (strong thud).
    3. The ticks should continue for the full duration of recording.
  • A3. Lock-zone enter/exit haptics fire.
    1. Long-press the shutter and start recording.
    2. While holding, drag your finger upward toward the lock target (the padlock icon area, ~95pt above shutter).
    3. Feel a light haptic as you cross into the armed zone (lock target highlights).
    4. Drag back down below the threshold. Feel another light haptic as you exit.
  • A4. Stop recording haptic fires; lock-engage haptic fires.
    1. Start a video recording (long-press). Release normally (below the lock zone). Feel a light haptic on stop.
    2. Start another recording. Drag up to lock target and release (lock engages). Feel a heavy haptic on lock engage.
    3. Tap the shutter (now showing a white square) to stop the locked recording. Feel a light haptic on stop.

B · Photo refresh (last-exercise spinner clears)

  • B1. Capture 2 photos — last one's spinner clears in-place without leaving Studio.
    1. Open a new session → Camera mode.
    2. Take a photo (tap shutter). Swipe to Studio — see the card with a spinner.
    3. Swipe back to Camera. Take a second photo.
    4. Swipe to Studio. Both exercise cards should show their thumbnails (spinners cleared). The LAST exercise card (the second photo) must not be stuck with a spinner.
    5. If the second card still shows a spinner, wait 3 seconds. If it doesn't clear, this is a FAIL.
  • B2. Capture 3 photos — all spinners clear without leaving Studio.
    1. Start a fresh session. Take 3 photos in quick succession (tap-swipe-tap-swipe-tap).
    2. Swipe to Studio. Wait up to 5 seconds for conversion to complete.
    3. All three exercise cards should show their thumbnails. No stuck spinners.

C · Treatment-aware thumbnails in Studio

  • C1. Photo exercise — flip treatment, thumbnail reflects the change.
    1. Open a session with at least one photo exercise.
    2. Expand the card. Tap the treatment segmented control (Line / B&W / Original tiles below the exercise name).
    3. Select B&W. The card thumbnail (88pt square on the left) should show a grayscale version of the raw photo.
    4. Select Original. The thumbnail should show the full-colour raw photo.
    5. Select Line. The thumbnail should show the line-drawing conversion (the default/existing look).
  • C2. Video exercise — flip treatment, thumbnail reflects the change.
    1. Open a session with at least one video exercise (fully converted).
    2. Expand the card. Flip to B&W.
    3. The card thumbnail should apply a grayscale filter (desaturated appearance).
    4. Flip to Original. The thumbnail returns to normal colour.
    5. Flip to Line. The thumbnail shows the line-drawing frame.
  • C3. Treatment change persists after navigating away and back.
    1. Set an exercise to Original treatment.
    2. Navigate back to the client list, then re-enter the session.
    3. The exercise card's thumbnail should still show the raw/colour thumbnail (reflecting Original treatment), not the line-drawing default.
