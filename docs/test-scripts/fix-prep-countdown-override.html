Device QA — Prep countdown respects session treatment override

Purpose: verify that flipping the lobby (or deck) gear popover's treatment pill (Line · B&W · Colour) now propagates to the prep countdown hero overlay too — not just the deck video and posters. Closes item 15 of the docs/test-scripts/2026-05-14 QA round.

Bug being fixed: PR #329 added a session-scoped treatment override that mirrors onto each slide's preferred_treatment. The video sources got re-bound via rebindVideoSources(), but the prep overlay's <img class="hero-poster" src=...>, baked at initial buildCard() time, was never refreshed. The big coral countdown number over the upcoming exercise's hero flashed the OLD treatment for one beat before the video took over with the NEW one.

Fix shape: new rebindPrepOverlays() helper in web-player/app.js walks every .prep-overlay in the card track and replaces it with fresh buildPrepOverlay(slide) markup. Preserves the [hidden] state and any in-flight countdown digit so the swap is invisible mid-prep. Called from applyTreatmentOverrideToAllExercises(), both body-focus toggles (deck + lobby), and the gear reset handler.

Invariant reinforced: 2026-05-14 hero treatment / no-fallback principle — when a slide is forced into a treatment, every surface that previews that slide (deck card, poster, prep overlay, lobby) must agree.

Out of scope: mobile preview tab playback (shares the same web-player/ bundle, gets the fix for free — verified by item 8 below), Studio card chrome, lobby row treatment rendering (already worked pre-fix).
