2026-05-15 · Studio card: equalise vertical spacing around title

Device QA — Studio card title spacing

Purpose: verify that the exercise title on the Studio card sits visually centred between the two elements that frame it — the media-type icon at the top-left of the text column (top element) and the state chip strip at the bottom of the text column (bottom element). Carl flagged the gap-above-title was different to the gap-below-title.

Fix: the text column's outer Column in app/lib/widgets/studio_exercise_card.dart switches from MainAxisAlignment.center to MainAxisAlignment.spaceBetween with three children: a top spacer that clears the media-type badge (29pt — matches the badge's 33pt container minus the 10pt column top-padding plus a small overshoot), the title block (title + plan summary + optional notes preview), and the state chip strip at the bottom. spaceBetween on three children distributes the free space equally between the two gaps, so the title block sits visually centred between top-element and bottom-element. Rest periods (no badge, no chip strip) keep MainAxisAlignment.center.

Out of scope: badge size, glyph, position; chip strip styling; title typography. Only the column-level vertical alignment changes.

Prerequisites

  • Build from fix/studio-card-title-spacing-equal branch installed on Carl's iPhone via ./install-device.sh.
  • One session with: (a) a video exercise in its default state (no notes, no special state chips), (b) a photo exercise with a notes line + body-focus toggled on (renders chip strip and adds the notes row), and (c) a rest period.
