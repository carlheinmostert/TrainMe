2026-05-13 · Photo Thumb Variants — Bundle 2b of audit

Device QA — Photo Three-Treatment Thumb Variant Pipeline (Bundle 2b · audit PR 6)

Purpose: Bundle 2b of the photo-vs-video treatment audit migration. Ships the symmetric photo thumbnail variant pipeline that the audit's F21 ("capture → convert → preview → publish breaks for photos") rooted to. Videos have produced three thumbnail variants (B&W _thumb.jpg, color _thumb_color.jpg, line _thumb_line.jpg) on disk since the 2026-05-05 Wave Three-Treatment-Thumbs wave; photos stamped thumbnailPath = rawFilePath directly with NO variant pipeline. The bridge's replaceFirst('_thumb.jpg', '_thumb_line.jpg') for hero_line / hero_color was a silent no-op for photos (the photo's thumbnailPath didn't end in _thumb.jpg), and the cloud get_plan_full RPC filtered the new thumbnail_url_* fields to media_type = 'video' exclusively.

Fixes in this PR:
· Step 1 (conversion): conversion_service.dart photo branch (around :325) now produces three thumbnail variants per photo capture via an OpenCV isolate (_extractPhotoThumbnailVariants). All three land in {Documents}/thumbnails/ with the same naming convention videos use. thumbnailPath points at the B&W variant (parity with video pipeline).
· Step 2 (SQLite): No new column needed. The single thumbnail_path column keeps holding the B&W variant; the other two are derived via filename replaceFirst exactly like the video pipeline does. No _dbVersion bump.
· Step 3 (bridge): unified_preview_scheme_bridge.dart _resolveMediaPath switch cases hero_line / hero_color work unchanged — the photo's thumbnailPath now ends in _thumb.jpg so the existing replaceFirst resolves correctly.
· Step 4 (cloud upload): upload_service.dart color-thumb upload loop (around :1707) widened from mediaType.name != 'video' to allow both video and photo. Defensive guards on the line-thumb backfill loops (:1004-1016, :1054-1075) skip when the replaceFirst would be a no-op (legacy photo rows pre-Bundle-2b).
· Step 4b (Supabase migration): supabase/migrations/20260513161415_photo_thumb_variants.sql re-creates get_plan_full with the thumbnail_url_line + thumbnail_url_color CASE arms widened from e.media_type = 'video' to e.media_type IN ('video', 'photo'). All other CASE arms preserved verbatim from the 2026-05-12 env-aware-thumb-line migration. Existence checks remain the gate.
· Step 5 (api.js): web-player/api.js _normaliseExercise at :253 now normalises thumbnail_url_line + thumbnail_url_color so cloud-fetched plans expose them to pickTreatmentPoster / resolveExerciseHero. Mirror at app/assets/web-player/api.js via dart run tool/sync_web_player_bundle.dart.

Backfill (Step 6): existing photos in the database do NOT have the new variant files. Option C accepted (smallest change). Future photo captures get the full three-treatment pipeline; existing photos render line-only treatment in their respective surfaces until re-captured. Documented as limitation, no eager backfill script. Carl can decide later if backfill is worth complexity.

R-10 parity: web-player/api.js mirrored to app/assets/web-player/api.js. diff shows no drift.

In scope: photo variant extraction (Dart isolate + OpenCV) · cloud upload symmetry · Supabase RPC media_type filter · api.js normalization · embedded + cloud surface photo treatments.

Out of scope: Bundle 2a's exercise_hero_resolver Flutter widget · person-crop refinement on photo thumbs (could be follow-up) · backfill of pre-Bundle-2b photos · web-portal · referral / payments.
