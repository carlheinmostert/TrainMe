Device QA — Wave 5

Purpose: verify the portal /members rewrite exposes real identities (email + name), owners can mint per-practitioner invite codes, unauthenticated landing on /join/:code gates through auth then claims the invite, and role-change + remove + leave flows enforce the blocked-cases (last owner, self-role-change, only-member leave).

In scope: /members table identity fix, invite minting + revocation, /join/:code flow (fresh signup + existing user + already-member + invalid code), role changes, remove, leave, mobile claim entry.

Out of scope: audit log expansion (covered in Wave 9), member avatars, in-app notifications of new joiners, email invites.

Prerequisites

  • Portal deployed with Wave 5 PR merged. Supabase migration for practice_invite_codes + 6 new RPCs applied.
  • Two practitioner test accounts available: one owner (Carl's main), one clean account with no practice membership yet.
  • A second practice where Carl is an owner with at least one other practitioner member, for role-change + remove + leave tests.

1 · Members list — identity surface

The scaffold shows truncated UUIDs. After the rewrite, email + full name come through via the new list_practice_members_with_profile RPC.

  • 1. Open manage.homefit.studio/members as an owner — table shows a row per member with Email, Name, Role, Joined date. No truncated UUID placeholders remain.
  • 2. Own row is tagged "(you)" and has a Leave practice button visible in the Actions column.
  • 3. Open /members as a non-owner practitioner — table renders but Role dropdown and Remove button are absent. Own row still shows Leave.

2 · Invite mint + copy

Owners mint a 7-char opaque code per invited practitioner. No expiry. One code per invited person.

  • 4. Click Invite a practitioner at the top of /members — a new 7-char code is generated, displayed, and the full https://manage.homefit.studio/join/{code} URL is copied to clipboard. Toast confirms.
  • 5. Mint two more codes in succession — each is unique, each uses the unambiguous alphabet (no 0/O/1/I/l). All three appear in a pending-invites list somewhere on the page.

3 · Claim flow — /join/:code

Landing page routes through auth and inserts the claimer as a practitioner member.

  • 6. Open /join/{code} in a private window (unauth) — page shows the practice name + a Sign in to join button. After email/password or magic-link sign-in, page auto-redirects back and claims the invite.
  • 7. After claim, redirect lands on /dashboard with the invited practice now selected in the switcher. Credit balance + clients list reflect the joined practice.
  • 8. Open /join/{already-claimed-code} — page shows a friendly "This invite was already used" message, no crash. Offer to sign in + navigate to dashboard.
  • 9. Open /join/{made-up-nonsense} — page shows "Invite not found or revoked" message, not a blank screen or stack trace.
  • 10. Sign in as a user who is already a member of the practice, then open /join/{valid-code-for-that-practice} — page shows "You're already a member" and lands on the practice dashboard. No duplicate row inserted in practice_members.

4 · Role changes (owner ↔ practitioner)

Owner-only control, DB-enforced blocks on last-owner demotion and self-role-change.

  • 11. On a practice with 2+ owners, change another owner's role to practitioner via the Role dropdown — update persists, table refreshes, their access in Portal is now practitioner-only on next page load.
  • 12. Try to demote yourself (own-row role dropdown) — the dropdown is disabled / not present. RPC called directly via browser console returns a guard error.
  • 13. On a practice where you are the only owner, try to demote yourself — blocked with a friendly "You are the last owner — promote someone first" message. No DB state change.

5 · Remove member

Owner-only. Hard-deletes the membership row. Revokes any unclaimed invite codes minted for them.

  • 14. Click Remove on a practitioner row — member disappears from the table immediately. Removed practitioner's next session refresh boots them out of that practice.
  • 15. Credit ledger entries attributed to the removed practitioner remain intact and still show the correct trainer_id (FK to auth.users, not the pivot row). Audit trail unbroken.

6 · Leave practice (self-service)

Any member can leave unless they're the last owner with practitioners remaining or the only member.

  • 16. As a practitioner in a multi-member practice, click Leave on own row — UndoSnackBar appears, dismissing the undo removes you; refreshing the portal shows the practice no longer in the switcher.
  • 17. As the only owner of a practice with practitioners remaining, click Leave — blocked with "Promote another owner before leaving" message. No membership row removed.
  • 18. As the only member of a practice, click Leave — blocked with "You're the only member — practice deletion is out of scope" or equivalent. No action.

7 · Mobile claim entry

Practitioner opens an invite URL on their iPhone → Safari → app should handle or direct to portal to claim, returning user to app with new practice membership visible.

  • 19. Tap a /join/{code} link on iPhone Safari as an already-signed-in practitioner — flow completes in Safari, portal confirms join, launch the homefit.studio app → new practice appears in the Practice Switcher bottom-sheet.
