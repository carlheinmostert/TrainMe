2026-05-15 · Editor sheet Demo tab + continuous swipe

Device QA — Editor Sheet Demo Tab + Continuous Swipe + Bottom-Aligned Content

What landed: two coordinated changes on the exercise editor sheet (ADR-0019 inversion extension).

(1) Demo tab. Tab strip renamed + reordered to Demo · Plan · Notes · Settings with Demo as the default opening tab. "Preview" was confusing — it implied passive viewing and clashed with the CAPS workflow's own "Preview" step. "Demo" matches the product narrative (line-drawing demonstrations).

(2) Continuous swipe + bottom-aligned content. Tab swipe is now one continuous PageView spanning every (exercise, tab) cell in the session — swipe past Settings of one exercise and you flow straight into the next exercise's Demo; swipe past the last exercise wraps to the first. Sheet stays parked at the 0.95 max detent (the 0.75 floor is still reachable by dragging the chrome down manually). Plan / Notes / Settings tab content is bottom-aligned — controls sit just above the tab strip for thumb reach, with empty space pushed to the top.

In scope: the editor sheet on every exercise type (photo / video / rest). Tab navigation, sheet detent behavior, chevron jumps, swipe-to-next-exercise overflow + wrap, bottom-aligned tab content visual check.

Out of scope: CAPS workflow Preview WebView (separate surface, unchanged); plan settings sheet's own tab strip (mirrors editor sheet only superficially); session shell PageView (Capture / Studio modes — different control).

Prerequisites

  • Build from feat/editor-sheet-demo-tab-and-swipe-overflow branch installed on Carl's iPhone via ./install-device.sh.
  • At least one client session with ≥ 3 exercises so wrap behaviour is observable (mix of video / photo / rest ideal).
  • Notes content on at least one exercise so the typing-persistence test is meaningful.

1 · Demo tab is the new default + tab strip order

Verify the rename + reorder + new default tab.

  • 1. Open Studio → tap any exercise card. Editor sheet opens on the Demo tab (active tab pill is the leftmost). Video starts playing inside the sheet.
  • 2. Tab strip reads exactly Demo · Plan · Notes · Settings left-to-right. No Preview label anywhere.
  • 3. From Studio, tap the Adjust button on the toolbar. Editor sheet opens on Demo for the last non-rest exercise.
  • 4. Tap each of the four tabs in turn. Each renders the right body: Demo shows the video; Plan shows the set rows + Add Set; Notes shows the textarea; Settings shows Prep + Reps in Video collapsibles.

2 · Sheet stays at 0.95 — floor reachable on demand

The old rule (Preview at 0.95, others at 0.75) is gone. The sheet always opens and stays at 0.95; the 0.75 floor is a deliberate drag-down destination.

  • 5. Open the sheet — it opens at the larger 0.95 detent (most of the screen, just a thin frosted strip of Studio visible above).
  • 6. Tap each tab in turn (Demo → Plan → Notes → Settings → Demo). Sheet height stays parked at 0.95 throughout — no oscillation between tabs.
  • 7. Drag the top drag-pill down slowly from the chrome. The sheet snaps to the 0.75 floor (more of Studio shows behind). Drag it back up — it snaps to 0.95 again.
  • 8. Tap the Notes tab → tap inside the textarea. Keyboard appears, sheet stays at 0.95, textarea remains visible (not eaten by the keyboard). Tap Done; keyboard dismisses; sheet stays at 0.95.

3 · Bottom-aligned tab content (one-handed reach)

The Plan / Notes / Settings tab content pins to the bottom — empty space sits at the top, controls are just above the tab strip in thumb range. Demo keeps its full-canvas video.

  • 9. On an exercise with only 1 or 2 sets, open the Plan tab. The "Add Set" button + caption + set rows sit at the bottom of the page (near the tab strip), not at the top. Empty space is at the top.
  • 10. On an exercise with no notes (or short notes), open the Notes tab. The textarea sits at the bottom of the page. Empty space is above.
  • 11. Open the Settings tab. Prep + Reps in Video rows sit at the bottom. Tap Prep — its preset chip row expands inline. Tap a chip — value commits, row collapses. Values feel comfortably reachable with the thumb.
  • 12. On a video exercise, open the Demo tab. The video fills the full upper canvas as before — no bottom-alignment, no empty space at the top. (Body Focus + Rotate pills bottom-left, treatment pill vertical right, tab strip + bottom rail below.)

4 · Continuous swipe — tab boundaries flow into next exercise

The killer feature. Swipe across tabs, watch one exercise's tabs spill smoothly into the next exercise's Demo.

  • 13. Open the sheet on the FIRST exercise. Swipe left through tabs (within the page body, not on the rail or chrome): Demo → Plan → Notes → Settings.
  • 14. At Ex1.Settings, swipe left one more time. The sheet flows smoothly into Ex2.Demo (the next exercise's Demo). Bottom rail thumbnail + title + meta updates to Ex2.
  • 15. Continue swiping left through Ex2's tabs. When you hit Ex2.Settings, the next swipe lands on Ex3.Demo (or Ex3.Rest if Ex3 is a rest period — see test 17).
  • 16. Keep swiping left until you reach the LAST exercise's Settings. One more swipe — sheet wraps back to the first exercise's Demo. Now swipe right (back) from Ex1.Demo — wraps to Last.Settings.
  • 17. If your session has a rest period: swipe to the rest exercise. Tab strip collapses to a single Rest pill. One more swipe past it lands on the NEXT exercise's Demo (rest contributes exactly 1 page in the stream).

5 · Chevrons + chrome swipe still do quick exercise jumps

Bottom-rail chevrons + horizontal drag on the chrome (drag pill OR rail surface) keep the 1-tap quick-jump-to-next-exercise behaviour. These DON'T wrap (they disable at the first / last exercise).

  • 18. On any exercise, tap the right chevron on the bottom rail. Sheet jumps to the next exercise's Demo (not the same tab you were on). Bottom rail updates.
  • 19. Tap left chevron. Jumps to previous exercise's Demo. At the FIRST exercise, the left chevron is disabled (greyed at 30% opacity). At the LAST exercise, the right chevron is disabled too.
  • 20. Horizontal swipe on the drag-pill at the top of the sheet, or on the empty space of the bottom rail (not on the title or chevrons). Jumps to next / previous exercise — same as the chevrons.

6 · Notes persistence + per-exercise state

Each exercise has its own Notes controller. Typing on one and navigating away then back should preserve the text.

  • 21. Open the sheet on Ex1. Tap Notes → type a few words. Swipe right (chevron) to Ex2 → swipe left back to Ex1. Notes still shows what you typed (and it's saved — closing the sheet and reopening still shows it).
  • 22. Open Plan on Ex1 → change a set's reps. Swipe to Ex2 → swipe back. Ex1's reps still reflect your change (committed to SQLite + Studio card already shows it on dismiss).

7 · Dismiss flows still work

Sanity check the existing dismissal paths haven't regressed.

  • 23. Tap outside the sheet (on the frosted-scrim background). Sheet dismisses cleanly. Studio screen behind looks normal.
  • 24. Open the sheet → fast-flick the drag pill downward (fling). Sheet dismisses. (Fast upward fling promotes to 0.95 if not already there.)
