2026-04-22 · Wave 18.10 — polish round 7

Device QA — Wave 18.10

Purpose: three more Studio-card polish items from Wave 18.9 live device QA. (1) Chevron realign. Wave 18.9's Montserrat 18pt header left the chevron's Wave-18.3-era top: 1 padding stranded — chevron floated high against the taller line box. Retuned to top: 5 (and the coral non-default dot bumped 11 → 13 to track). (2) Asymmetric section-header padding. Wave 18.6's symmetric vertical: 2 made section breaks feel like continuations of the previous body. Now top: 12, bottom: 2 — generous breathing room ABOVE each header, tight space below. First header (PLAYBACK) passes isFirst: true to suppress the top-12 (card's own top inset already covers it). (3) Expanded-body tray. New _ExpandedBody wrapper gives every open accordion section a darker recessed fill (surfaceBg vs the card's surfaceBase) + 2pt coral hairline on the left edge. Signals "this is the live drawer". All hierarchy from Wave 18.9 (Montserrat 18pt header / 12pt inner tier / stacked editors) preserved.

In scope: mobile-only. One file — studio_exercise_card.dart (chevron padding retune + isFirst param + asymmetric header padding + new _ExpandedBody wrapper around all four bodies).

Out of scope: anything already covered by Wave 18 / 18.1 through 18.9. Web player. Portal. Font sizes / weights unchanged. Storage semantics unchanged. InlineNumericEditor / _ControlRow / _PrepSecondsRow / _DurationPerRepRow internals unchanged — only the containers around them.

Prerequisites

  • Wave 18.9 merged. Wave 18.10 branch fix/wave-18.10-polish installed via ./install-device.sh.
  • At least one session with: (a) a video exercise with at least one non-default value (e.g. reps=10 or preferred treatment set) so the coral non-default dot appears on a group header; (b) NOTES with multi-paragraph text to confirm its expanded body wraps cleanly inside the recessed tray.
  • iPhone 17 Pro or similar 393pt-wide device.

1 · Chevron + coral dot realign

  • 1. Open any exercise card. On every collapsed section header (PLAYBACK / PLAN / PACING / NOTES), the leading chevron icon visually aligns with the middle of the Montserrat 18pt label line box — no more floating high above the letterforms. Compare to Wave 18.9 where the chevron was sitting roughly at the cap-height top. Check all four headers. If a header has a coral non-default dot (trailing), the dot also aligns — chevron and dot read as a pair on the label's cap-height centre.

2 · Asymmetric header padding

  • 2. Expand any one group (say PLAN). The gap BETWEEN the end of the expanded PLAN body and the NEXT section header (PACING) feels like a real section break — ~12pt of breathing room. Wave 18.8's tight run-on feel is gone. Conversely, the gap between PLAN's own header and its expanded body stays tight (~2pt below the header — Wave 18.8 behaviour preserved). PLAYBACK (the first header) sits close to the card's top edge with no double-padding — its isFirst: true suppresses the top-12 that other headers get.

3 · Recessed expanded-body tray

  • 3. Expand PLAN. The expanded body (REPS / SETS / HOLD rows and their chip rails) sits on a visibly DARKER fill than the rest of the card. The contrast is subtle but clearly a distinct "tray" — the card's own surfaceBase (#1A1D27) vs the body's surfaceBg (#0F1117, matching the app root). Content reads as contained. Same treatment on PLAYBACK, PACING, NOTES.
  • 4. Expand PLAN. The LEFT edge of the expanded body has a 2pt vertical stroke running its full height, coloured muted coral (coral at ~45% alpha — subtle, not screaming). Confirms "this section is open / live". Only the left edge — no border on top, right, or bottom. Same treatment on PLAYBACK, PACING, NOTES when expanded.

4 · Accordion rhythm preserved

  • 5. Rapid-switch between sections: tap PLAYBACK (opens); tap PLAN (PLAYBACK closes, PLAN opens with its recessed+hairline body); tap NOTES (PLAN closes, NOTES opens; TextField editable inside the recessed body). Transitions feel smooth — no flash, no layout jump beyond the body itself swapping. Text field in NOTES still captures input. All Wave 18.9 hierarchy (Montserrat 18pt header / 12pt inner tier / stacked editors in PACING) preserved.
