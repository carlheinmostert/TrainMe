# TrainMe Market Research

*Compiled 2026-04-16*

## Competitive Landscape

The market splits into rehab-focused platforms (Physitrack, Medbridge) and fitness-focused platforms (TrueCoach, PT Distinction). Almost all rely on pre-built exercise video libraries.

| Platform | Price | Library | Custom Content | Delivery | Target |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Physitrack | $22/mo | 17,000+ videos | Upload | PhysiApp | Physio, OT |
| Medbridge | $149-425/yr | 8,000+ | Upload | App, email, SMS | Physio (US) |
| Rehab My Patient | ~$14/mo | 5,500+ | Yes | Email, SMS, WhatsApp, PDF | Physio, chiro |
| HEP2go | Free/$5/mo | Thousands | Upload | Email, SMS, QR, print | PT, OT |
| TrueCoach | $25-110/mo | 3,500+ | Upload | Client app | Personal trainers |
| PT Distinction | $20-60/mo | 1,200+ | Upload | Branded app | Personal trainers |
| Exercise.com | $125/mo | Large | Upload + branded app | Custom app | Gyms |
| Groovi (SA) | R335/mo | 1,000+ | Limited | Cloud web | SA physio, bios |

### Key Findings

1. **Nobody does on-device visual conversion** of trainer footage
2. **Groovi is the only SA competitor** targeting biokineticists (pre-built library, no custom capture)
3. **WhatsApp delivery is rare** (only Rehab My Patient)
4. Common pain points: stock libraries lack specialty exercises, desktop-centric workflows, client app fatigue

### TrainMe Differentiators

1. In-session capture-to-demo pipeline (unique)
2. Line drawing de-identification (POPIA by design)
3. No library dependency (every exercise is custom)

## Business Case Validation

### The Compliance Problem

- 50-70% non-compliance with home exercise programs
- Only 35% of patients fully adhere to plans of care
- Top barriers: low self-efficacy, forgetting exercises

### Visual Materials Impact

- Compliance rose from **38.1% to 77.4%** when visual take-home materials were provided alongside verbal instruction (2x improvement)
- Video demonstrations improve exercise reproduction accuracy vs static images
- Note: evidence for improved clinical outcomes (not just adherence) is still developing

### Therapist Behaviour

- "Lack of time" is the #1 barrier to creating visual exercise materials
- Existing HEP software described as clunky and frustrating
- Therapists already film exercises on patients' phones during sessions (validates our workflow)

### South African Context

- 90% of SA healthcare workers use messaging apps for work
- 84% use WhatsApp specifically, two-thirds daily
- WhatsApp delivery is table stakes for SA market

### Pitch Guidance

Lead with adherence improvement (38% to 77%) and correct exercise execution. Do not claim improved health outcomes directly until more evidence supports that link. The tool removes friction between filming (which therapists already do) and distributing professional plans via the channel SA already uses.
