Why stack? A circuit is a short loop — presenting cycles as additional rows under the same columns preserves spatial memory: the bio sees at a glance "I'm on round 2, looping back to exercise A1". The coral tint band (brand.tint-bg, 12% alpha) visually groups what scrolls together.

05Auto-scroll

Active pill always sits at viewport centre. Completed pills fade off the left edge; upcoming stay full-strength on the right.

FRAME A · active = exercise 2 of 12

Early in plan — pill 1 already off-left (fading), pill 2 centred under the pulse, pills 3–12 read at full strength.

FRAME B · active = exercise 8 of 12
‹

Mid-plan — pills 1–6 fade off-left (inside the 40px gradient mask), pill 8 centred, 9–12 full strength on right. Small coral chevron at left hints "scrub back to active" after manual swipe. Auto-snap-back after 4s.

Rule: active pill always centred · completed flow off the left (40px linear fade to surface.bg) · upcoming stays full-strength on the right (no fade) · circuit rows scroll in lockstep with row 1 because they share columns · translate animated 300ms cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1).

06Long-press peek panel

Long-press a pill to preview it, slide to scan, release to jump.

Bent-over row
3 × 12 · hold 2s
release to jump here
ROW
Gesture chain: long-press any pill → peek panel rises 80px above showing thumbnail, name, settings. Slide finger left/right — peek follows the pill under finger, haptic selection click per pill crossed. Release on a new pill = jump + reset timer. Release on original pill = peek dismisses, nothing changes. Active pill's timer fill freezes at its current position for the duration of the press; resumes or resets on release.

07Interaction rules

Complete list of gestures, their behaviours, and open questions.

Tap pill
No-op for now. Post-mockup decision: either no-op or open the expanded exercise card (same as the carousel card tap).
Long-press pill
Open peek panel (thumbnail + name + settings + "release to jump" hint). Freezes the active pill's timer fill for the press duration.
Long-press + slide
Peek follows the pill under the finger. Haptic selectionClick fires each time a new pill is crossed.
Release on new pill
That pill becomes active. Its timer resets to 0 and starts counting. The matrix auto-scrolls to re-centre it (300ms emphasized).
Release on original pill
Peek dismisses. Timer fill resumes from its frozen position. No navigation.
Swipe matrix left/right
Manual scrub — scan ahead or back without navigating. A pulsing coral chevron appears near the appropriate edge pointing to the active pill. Auto-snap-back to centre after 4 seconds idle.
Completed pill recolour
Muted ink fill, not coral. Completed should read as "cooled down, done, relax" — not as "you're here".
Rest pills
Same shape + dual-channel active behaviour, but everything coral becomes blue-grey (semantic.rest). Rest is a distinct category, not an accent variant.
Circuit band
Coral tint background (brand.tint-bg, 12% alpha) behind all rows of a circuit's columns. Purely a grouping affordance — no interaction.
Dense tier pill hit area
Even though the dense pill is 24×12, the hit rectangle extends 8px on all sides so fat-finger scrubbing works on small plans too.
