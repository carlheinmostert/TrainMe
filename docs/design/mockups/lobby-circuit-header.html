KEY ARCHITECTURAL DIFFERENCE vs hotfix round 2:
The rail pieces are REAL elements inside `.ex-row-gutter`, NOT pseudo-
elements on `.ex-row`. The gutter is a real flex column outside the
card's bounding box. This means the active-row coral border (intentional
UX — focused row gets a coral outline) sits on the CONTENT card and
naturally wraps around just the card; the rail in the gutter column is
unaffected. With pseudo-elements at `left: 8px`, the rail was rendered
INSIDE the row's box and got covered by the card border / any z-stacking
of overlay layers.

What changed (hotfix round 3)

  • Two-column rows. Each row is now a real flex container with a fixed 22 px gutter column on the left and a flex content column on the right. The rail pieces are real <span> children of the gutter, NOT absolutely-positioned pseudo-elements on the row. Result: the rail sits in its own layout slot OUTSIDE the card's bounding box, so the active-row coral border (intentional UX) wraps around just the card and the rail is unaffected.
  • Container. .lobby-circuit-group stays transparent (no backdrop, no border). All grouping is conveyed by the rail.
  • Rail tokens. 3 px wide, pinned to left: 11px within the 22 px gutter (sits at the gutter's right edge so the line lands just outside the card column). Colour rgba(255,107,53,0.85) (use --c-coral-rail).
  • Header rail piece. .circuit-header-gutter-rail = centre → bottom of header. .circuit-header-gutter-connector = 11 × 3 horizontal stub at vertical centre, joining rail to label.
  • Per-row connectors. .ex-row-gutter-rail = full-height vertical piece. .ex-row-gutter-connector = 11 × 3 stub at row centre. Adjacent in-circuit cards collapse top borders (margin-top: -1px) so the rail reads as one continuous coral line.
  • Last-row L. .ex-row.in-circuit.last .ex-row-gutter-rail sets height: 50% instead of top:0; bottom:0 so the rail terminates at the row centre — combined with the connector at that centre line, it forms a clean └ corner.
  • Numerals. Removed entirely. Default-named exercises now have a fallback “Exercise N” label generated in lobby.js from a position counter, since Studio doesn’t persist the auto-name to the cloud row. Renamed exercises trust the practitioner.
  • Cycles chip. “× 3 ROUNDS” → ×3. Just the multiplier.
