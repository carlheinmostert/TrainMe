One detail — what if circuit cycles = 1?

If ×1 we render 1 box, ×2 → 2 boxes, ×N → N boxes (capped at some sensible max, probably 5 or 6 before it gets silly).

Same animation applies regardless of N. The ripple variants (1, 2) get more visually interesting with more boxes; the synchronous variant (3) reads the same at any N.
