Exercise card v2 · tabbed bottom-sheet editor

Replaces the inline-accordion exercise card. The card collapses to a single compact row (thumb · title · Dose / Notes triggers · gear). Editing happens in a tabbed bottom sheet with two detents — full DOSE table, freeform Notes, client-eye Preview, and Settings. Each trigger lands the sheet on its home tab; swipe horizontally or tap to switch.

Pyramid sets fan out in the Dose summary. Notes summary truncates. Gear sits muted at far right because no settings deviate from defaults.
