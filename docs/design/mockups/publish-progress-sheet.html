Note: Chip is non-blocking, lives under the AppBar (above the exercise list). Spinner indicates progress. Tapping anywhere on the chip re-opens the full sheet. On completion, chip morphs into the dismissible "Plan published" toast; on failure, chip turns coral with "Tap to retry".

Design decisions captured

  • (1) Non-blocking — swipe-dismissible sheet; publish continues in background; chip on the workflow toolbar surfaces progress so the user can re-open the sheet at any time.
  • (2) Aggregated progress — Phase 3 (Uploading treatments) shows "N of M files" + a thin coral progress bar. Per-file granularity is one tap away (failure-state diagnostic).
  • (3) Failure UX — failing row turns coral, halts subsequent rows, credit refunds silently, big retry button at the bottom, deep diagnostic tap-through on the failed row's "Show which files →" link.
  • (4) Success — 1-second "All set" beat, then sheet auto-dismisses. Share affordance lights up on the workflow toolbar.
  • (5) Cancellation — not offered. Swipe-dismiss continues the publish; no abort path. Avoids the race between credit consume and refund.
  • (6) Phase count — 5 visible phases. Atomic and fast phases (credit reserve, plan save, finalise) get their own row; slow phase (uploads) gets the progress detail.
