Wave 38 · design mockup

Studio · bottom-toolbar refactor

Reachability play. Studio is the practitioner's primary work surface; today all its chrome lives at the top, away from the thumb. Three changes:

(1) Session name relocates from top of Studio to a muted subtitle near the bottom toolbar — practitioner can verify "what am I editing?" without backing out.
(2) Session name becomes inline-editable on the SessionCard on the client detail page (mirror the client-name pattern: dashed underline, tap to edit, onSubmitted fires the rename RPC, no modals).
(3) Toolbar + stats move to a fixed bottom bar. Same items as today's top toolbar (back, preview, publish). When the plan is locked, the unlock pill appears right-aligned on the toolbar line.

Open questions

1. Where does the unlock pill go in the toolbar layout? In the locked mockup it sits right-aligned, replacing the publish button (since publish costs a credit when locked, the unlock CTA logically gates the publish path). Alternative: keep publish visible AND add unlock to its right, so the user can see both. Pick a behaviour.

2. Padlock indicator separate from the unlock pill? Today the AppBar has a padlock chip + an unlock CTA inside its sheet. In this mockup the lock state is communicated via the colour-coded dot in the stats strip + the unlock pill in the toolbar. Padlock chip is retired. Confirm this matches your intent.

3. Top-of-list spacing. With no AppBar above, the first exercise card sits at the very top edge of the safe area. Want a small (~12pt) breathing space, or full-bleed against the status bar's bottom edge?

4. Auto-hide on scroll? Iconic Apple-canon pattern (Safari, Notes) hides the bottom bar on scroll-down + reveals on scroll-up. Adds polish but more state to manage. Default-fixed is simpler. Pick one.

5. Session subtitle truncation. "26 Apr 2026 · New client f068" fits on a 6.1" iPhone width but a renamed session ("Lower body recovery — Lauren post-knee-op week 4") will overflow. Ellipsise from the right, drop the date, or wrap to two lines? Simplest: ellipsise (single line stays clean).

Mockup uses the canonical brand tokens (coral #FF6B35, surface #0F1117, raised #171923). Toolbar buttons sized to 44pt minimum tap targets per Apple HIG. Iconography is placeholder; final glyphs pulled from the existing AppBar.
